About the project

Smarter policing,
powered by data.

CrimeGuard AI is a smart tool that analyses crime data to predict how likely a case is to be resolved — and helps police make sharper decisions in seconds.

Why this matters

Crime is rising, and police resources are limited. Officers can't be everywhere at once. CrimeGuard AI helps investigators focus on the cases that need attention most — spotting high-risk situations early, allocating officers where they'll make the biggest difference, and surfacing the environmental factors (lighting, CCTV, suspect visibility) that make cases solvable.

How it works

1. Enter Details

Provide the essential details required for analysis.

2. AI Analyses

The model evaluates all inputs and estimates the likelihood of case resolution.

3. Get Results

Receive the predicted outcome, risk level, officer recommendation, and actionable investigation insights.

What you'll get out of it

Instant prediction of the likelihood that a case will be resolved.
Receive AI-powered recommendations for officer allocation.
Actionable recommendations to improve area safety — better lighting, more CCTV, patrol frequency.

Disclaimer

CrimeGuard AI supports investigative decision-making through AI-powered case analysis. While the system provides data-driven predictions and recommendations, final operational decisions should always rely on verified evidence, official protocols, and professional judgement.
